About Us
Head of School Welcome

It is with great delight that I welcome you to the website of Bali Bilingual International School (BBIS) and to the wonderful vibrant island of Bali.

As a newly established school, BBIS aims to earn the reputation as the provider of top-quality education to children of both expat and local families on Bali Island. We are also very proud to be one of three schools in Bali to offer all 3 IB International Baccalaureate Organization (IBO) programmes: the Primary Years Programme, the Middle Years Programme, and the Diploma Programme.

Additionally, we are a fully accredited school with the Western Association with Schools and Colleges and the Council of International Schools. Being accredited signifies compliance with rigorous, high-quality educational standards and provides our graduates access to many of the world’s premier international universities.

To further promote international-mindedness and global citizenship, our school calendar is filled with a diverse and engaging selection of academic and non-academic celebrations, festivals, and other culturally enriching events. In addition to the rich curriculum on offer at BBIS, we also provide a varied co-curricular activities programme after school to enrich and enhance the students’ learning experiences at BBIS.

As the Head of School, I warmly invite you to visit our campus and discover our state-of-the-art facilities, welcoming classroom environments, and sustainable grounds. This visit will offer you a firsthand look at the safe and enriching educational experiences available to your child at BBIS. I would be delighted to personally guide you through our campus and discuss our IB programmes and the significance of being an accredited school. Please contact our office team today to schedule your visit.

Aldaine Hunt
Head of School
